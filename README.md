# Tennessee Socialist Rifle Association Sign-up
1. Sign-up as a member of the [National SRA](https://socialistra.org/)

2. As you fill out the National's form, there is a *Chapter Preference* option. Choose Tennessee

3. Fill out this form.

4. You can message us via our [Instagram Page](https://www.instagram.com/tennesseesra/) and we should reach out within 4-5 business days

5. Finally, you will be set up for a meeting with some members who will guide you through your cadre process!

# FAQ
* Do I need to own firearms to join?
  - No, you do not need to own firearms.

* I am not quite sure that I am a *leftist*, but I am interested in firearms and want a safe community to learn with. Can I still join?
  - Of course! We are a *Big-Tent* organization and are focused on supporting our community, first and foremost.

* Is there a place for me if I am two-hours from Nashville?
  - Absolutely! Tennessee has three communities in the SRA: West, Central, and East. There are TN Chapter events *and* separate events for.
